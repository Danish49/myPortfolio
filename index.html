About Me

Danish Mushtaq is a software developer, computer science teacher, and entrepreneur based in Kashmir, India. With a strong foundation in web development and a passion for technology, he has dedicated his career to building practical digital solutions and sharing knowledge through teaching. As a computer science educator, Danish focuses on empowering students with real-world skills and a deep understanding of programming and problem-solving. Alongside his work in education and software development, Danish is also the founder of a growing e-commerce clothing brand aimed at delivering quality, culturally rooted fashion to a modern audience. His entrepreneurial journey reflects his commitment to innovation, purpose-driven branding, and customer-focused business development. Danish's long-term vision is to build a successful international business that blends technology, design, and identity. He continues to pursue excellence in all aspects of his work, bridging the gap between tech and entrepreneurship with creativity and determination.
